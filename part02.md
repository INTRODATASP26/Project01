PART 2

As I remembered my own musical taste and how it changed, I first thought about Spotify Wrapped. 

I hate Spotify Wrapped! It always comes out wrong, somehow. Or at least I felt it was wrong. I always track my listening through third party apps, like stats for spotify and last.fm, and it always comes out SO OFF. I figured it would be interesting to use the data to try redoing some Wrapped-esque stats, because I always felt something off about them - foreshadowing.

I did struggle around figuring out the logic to setup (especially because I chose to start a project on VScode this time and realized I had to download and setup a lot of modules), but having the logic down the process was a bit more striaght forward. Not doing math, yay! I end up using Claude as an aid, but I did try to at least pseudocode and get errors for two or three times before going for help.

The process was fun! I ended up messing with some yearly statistics in the Wrapped spirit, but I also decided on making an autopsy of how my current obsession with Stray Kids (my top artist from 2025) happened. Doing that, I was testing a monthly ranking of my hours spent listening to Stray Kids and also realized Spotify had a bunch of blind spots! I asked for my entire data, but somehow I only got September and August. I even tried to search in the files themselves, but oh well. 

At least it explains to me why my Spotify Wrapped always seems off.
