Reflections - Project 1

PART 1

I listen to a lot of music. Spotify is (and has been for a few years) my most used apps. So, I decided to extract my spotify data and play around with how much I am obsessed with music. I was between that and extracting my AO3 (archive or our own, a fanfic website) data, which is another website I use a lot and since it comes from an open archive, there are many ways to either collect it manually or scrap using Phyton scripts, but I didn't want to expose myself too much.

I used Spotify own Data Collection tool. Supposedly it comes with every streaming data from the moment you opened your account (foreshadowing). It was a pretty easy process, it just took 5 days for the files to come through email.

Overall, I found the data pretty interesting. It is very thorough. It shows information such as the time a stream ended, the song id, if you were using shuffle (!) or the country you were streaming from. I ended up scrolling through a bunch of my data from 2026 all the way back to 2016, having a little trip memory lane remembering my musical hyperfixations, the first song I streamed, things like that. It was nice remembering.
